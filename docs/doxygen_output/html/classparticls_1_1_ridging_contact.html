Detailed Description

template<unsigned int dim>
class particls::RidgingContact< dim >

A viscoelastic-plastic contact law to model sea ice ridging.

A viscoelastic-plastic contact law to model sea ice ridging based on the paper "On the mesoscale interaction of lead ice and floes" by Mark Hopkins.
